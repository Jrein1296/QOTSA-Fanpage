Bio

Queens of the Stonage were born in a different time; a time when shredding guitars vanquished their enemies. A time when men with beards and flannel shirts wielded huge double-bit felling axes; they knew nothing of craft beers and hard lemonade and were all the better for it. A time when pop musicians were still embarrased to be themselves.

Who is in the band?

  1. Josh Homme on guitar and vocals
  2. Troy Van Leuwen on guitar since 2002. Josh handled guitar on his own 'til then.
  3. Dave Grohl played drums for one year, he's great and all, but no match for...
  4. Jon Theodore, who absolutely slays the skins.
  5. Nick Oliveri was on bass until '04. He couldn't keep his shit together. So, along came...
  6. Michael Shuman. He's like Nick but with 37% extra funk and 100% extra hair.
